# EarnYourScreen — 30-Second Promo Video Script

**Format:** Play Store promo video  
**Length:** 30 seconds  
**Tone:** Energetic, fun, Duolingo-ad energy  
**Target:** Parents of K–12 students

---

## FULL SCRIPT

---

### SEGMENT 1 — THE PROBLEM (0:00 – 0:05)

**Scene:**  
Quick-cut montage. Kid on couch, phone in hand, blank-staring at TikTok. Parent walks in, crosses arms, mouths "homework?" Kid shrugs. Parent sighs.

**On-Screen Text:**  
> *"Sound familiar?"*

**Voiceover:**  
> "Your kid wants their phone. You want their homework done. The battle never ends."

---

### SEGMENT 2 — SOLUTION REVEAL (0:05 – 0:15)

**Scene:**  
Phone screen fills the frame. Instagram icon appears with a lock overlay and a glow effect. The lock interstitial screen slides in — "Answer 3 questions to unlock." Kid leans forward, interested. Taps "Start Quiz."

**On-Screen Text:**  
> *"Introducing EarnYourScreen"*  
> *"Unlock time. Earn it first."*

**Voiceover:**  
> "EarnYourScreen locks their favorite apps until they answer a few questions. No fighting. No nagging. Just math, science, or whatever grade they're in — right before they scroll."

---

### SEGMENT 3 — FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS (0:15 – 0:25)

**Scene:**  
Fast sequence of in-app moments, each lasting ~2 seconds:

1. **Quiz screen** — kid taps "12" on the square root question. The card flashes green.  
   *On-Screen Text: "Real curriculum questions · K–12"*

2. **Unlock success screen** — big teal checkmark, "+30 XP" in amber pops on screen.  
   *On-Screen Text: "Earn XP. Level up. Unlock."*

3. **Streak badge** — "🔥 5 Day Streak!" celebrates on screen with a bounce animation.  
   *On-Screen Text: "Build a learning streak"*

4. **Parent dashboard** — parent on a different device sets Instagram to "3 questions, Math, Grade 7." Taps save.  
   *On-Screen Text: "Parents control everything"*

**Voiceover:**  
> "Kids earn XP, build streaks, and unlock apps they actually want. Parents set the subject, the grade, and exactly how many questions to answer."

---

### SEGMENT 4 — CTA (0:25 – 0:30)

**Scene:**  
App icon animation — lock icon transforms into a glowing star. EarnYourScreen logo on a purple background. Google Play badge fades in.

**On-Screen Text (large, centered):**  
> *"EarnYourScreen"*  
> *"Free on Google Play"*

**Voiceover:**  
> "EarnYourScreen. Free on Google Play. Download it today — your kids might actually enjoy doing homework."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Music | Upbeat, punchy, game-like (think Duolingo / mobile game tutorial SFX) |
| Pacing | Cut on every beat; no shot longer than 2.5s except the CTA |
| Color grade | Warm, saturated — match brand (#7F77DD, #EF9F27, #1D9E75) |
| Aspect ratio | 16:9 (landscape) for Play Store promo video |
| Voice | Friendly female or gender-neutral VO; energetic but not hyped |
| Devices | Show both a kid's phone and a parent's phone to reinforce dual-user design |
| Supers | All on-screen text in white or XP Amber on purple backgrounds |
